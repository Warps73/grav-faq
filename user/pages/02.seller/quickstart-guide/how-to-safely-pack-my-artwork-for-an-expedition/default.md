Artmajeur is an Online Art Marketplace, it is much like an online art gallery, except that sellers and contact artists directly.

**For who?**
Artmajeur is aimed art lovers and collectors interested in Art of all styles, techniques and prices, by artists from all over the world.


**How it works ?**
The buyer interested in a work contacts the sellers directly. Artmajeur secure the purchase and garantees that the seller will be paid, and the buyer will actually receive its artworks.


**What makes Artmajeur unique?**
Artmajeur finally democratizes the purchase of art by offering direct and secure access to artists from all over the world. Transactions are really secure (all transactions are made using 3DSecure protocol) and really direct (Art is shipped right the artist's galleries or studio).


**How much does it cost to sell?**
It is 100% free to create an account and present artworks for sale! Artmajeur will only get paid when sales are made.
